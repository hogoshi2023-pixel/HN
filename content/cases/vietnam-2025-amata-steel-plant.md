# Project Overview

Full anti-corrosion coating package for 12,000 m² of structural steel at the Amata Industrial Park steel plant, including girders, trusses, columns, crane beams, and pipe racks.

## Coating System

- **Primer:** HY-AC-201 zinc-rich epoxy (80% Zn content, 60 μm DFT)
- **Mid-coat:** HY-AC-302 high-build epoxy (100 μm DFT)
- **Topcoat:** HY-AC-405 acrylic polyurethane (60 μm DFT)
- **Total DFT:** 220 μm, exceeds ISO 12944 C4 spec

## Schedule

45 days surface prep → primer → mid-coat → topcoat → final DFT logging. All work completed on schedule despite monsoon-season humidity challenges.
