# Project Overview

18,000 m² warehouse floor coating for a new logistics hub. RAL 7035 light grey, R12 anti-skid finish, 5-year warranty against delamination.

## Technical Challenge

Standard epoxy requires cool, dry conditions for proper cure. Indonesia's 32°C + 90% humidity environment needed accelerated hardener. Our R&D team adjusted the hardener ratio to achieve 4-hour full cure at ambient tropical conditions.

## Result

Full project delivered in 28 days, no delays. Floor remains pristine after 12 months of forklift traffic.
