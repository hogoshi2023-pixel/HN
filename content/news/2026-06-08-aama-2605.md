# AAMA 2605 vs 2604 vs 2603

These three specs from the American Architectural Manufacturers Association define exterior coating performance. Picking wrong costs you 5–15 years of facade life.

## Performance Comparison

| Test | 2603 | 2604 | 2605 |
|------|------|------|------|
| Service Life | 1 yr | 5 yr | 10+ yr |
| Q-UV Exposure | 500 h | 1000 h | 2000 h+ |
| Salt Spray | 500 h | 1000 h | 2000 h+ |
| Humidity | 1500 h | 3000 h | 4000 h |
| Color Retention | moderate | high | very high |
| Cost Index | 1.0x | 1.4x | 1.8x |

## Specifying for Tropical / Coastal

For projects in Vietnam, Indonesia, Saudi Arabia, Philippines — go **2605 minimum**. Anything less fades fast under UV + salt.
