ORDER & LOGISTICS · 订单与物流
Q01. What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
起订量是多少？
+
Standard MOQ is 200 kg per product, with mixed colors welcome in the same shipment. For trial orders or new distributor relationships, we accept 50 kg samples. Custom formulations require a 500 kg minimum for the first production batch.
Q02. What is your lead time for production and shipping?
生产和发货周期多久？
+
Standard catalog products: 7–15 working days after deposit. Custom formulations: 30–45 days including sample approval. Shipping (sea FCL to most Asian ports): 5–10 days. To Middle East / Africa: 18–25 days. To South America / Europe: 28–35 days. Express samples via DHL: 3–5 days worldwide.
Q03. What packaging options do you offer?
包装规格有哪些？
+
5 L tin (≈5 kg, sample size) · 20 L drum (≈25 kg, standard export) · 200 L drum (≈250 kg, bulk) · 1000 L IBC tote (industrial orders). UN-certified packaging available for hazardous formulas. Custom drum labels with your brand available from 200 kg order.
Q04. What Incoterms do you support?
支持哪些贸易术语？
+
EXW Shenzhen · FOB Shenzhen · CIF destination port · DDP (selected countries with our logistics partner). We ship via Maersk, MSC, CMA-CGM, COSCO with long-term contracts for stable rates. LCL consolidation available for orders under 5 tonnes.
Q05. What payment methods do you accept?
接受哪些付款方式？
+
T/T (30% deposit, 70% before shipment — most common) · L/C at sight (for orders >USD 50,000) · Open Account for vetted distributors after 6-month trading history. Currencies: USD, EUR, RMB. Citibank Singapore USD account for international wires.
QUALITY & WARRANTY · 质量与质保
Q06. What quality certifications do your products carry?
产品有哪些认证？
+
ISO 9001:2015 (quality management) · ISO 14001:2015 (environmental) · CE marking for EU export · SGS third-party test reports available · Class A fire rating per ASTM E84 · Low-VOC grades <50 g/L · REACH / RoHS compliant on request · NSF/ANSI 61 for drinking water contact.
Q07. What warranty do you provide?
质保期多久？
+
Standard 24-month warranty from shipment date for product defects and batch inconsistency. Project-specific performance warranties (5–25 years depending on system and environment) available with on-site application by certified applicators.
Q08. Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?
可以先寄样品吗？
+
Yes. Free samples (1–2 kg) for stocked catalog colors shipped via DHL within 48 hours — you pay shipping (≈USD 30–80 depending on destination). Custom color samples: USD 80–150 per formulation, refundable against bulk order.
Q09. How do you ensure batch-to-batch consistency?
如何保证批次一致性？
+
Every batch is tested in our in-house QC lab against 60+ parameters. Batch retention samples archived for 24 months. COA (Certificate of Analysis) emailed before shipment. Statistical process control (SPC) on critical parameters. SGS pre-shipment inspection available on request (buyer pays).
CUSTOM & OEM · 定制与贴牌
Q10. Can you match a custom color (RAL, Pantone, or sample)?
可以定制颜色吗？
+
Yes. Send RAL / Pantone code or a physical sample (≥5 cm²). Lab color match in 3 days, production batch in 7–10 days. Color tolerance: ΔE ≤ 1.0 (spectrophotometer verified). Metallic, pearlescent, and textured finishes available.
Q11. Can you develop a fully custom formulation?
可以定制配方吗？
+
Yes — 12-chemist R&D team handles custom formulas. Send us your substrate, environment, performance targets (temperature, chemical resistance, hardness, color, etc.). Sample in 7 days, full production in 30 days. We sign NDAs and IP agreements for proprietary formulas.
Q12. How does OEM/private label work?
贴牌定制怎么操作？
+
Choose from 200+ existing formulas OR develop your own. We manufacture with your branded drum labels, packaging, MSDS in your languages. Minimum first order: 500 kg per SKU. Free design service for drum artwork (you provide logo & brand colors).
TECHNICAL & APPLICATION · 技术与施工
Q13. Do you provide on-site application support?
提供现场施工指导吗？
+
For orders above USD 50,000 or projects above 5,000 m², our application engineers can visit your site for surface prep specification, applicator training, and QA supervision. Travel & accommodation billed at cost; service hours are complimentary.
Q14. How should paint be stored in tropical climates?
热带气候涂料如何储存？
+
Store in shade at 5–35°C, away from direct sunlight and ignition sources. Keep drums sealed until use. Rotate FIFO (first-in-first-out). Shelf life: 12–24 months depending on product. For warehouses above 40°C, climate control recommended.
Q15. What's the right DFT for my project?
我的项目应该用多厚的涂层？
+
Depends on ISO 12944 corrosivity category. C2 (rural): 120 μm. C3 (urban/light industrial): 160 μm. C4 (industrial/coastal): 200 μm. C5-I (heavy industrial): 260 μm. C5-M (marine): 320 μm. Send your environment — we'll specify the exact system.
Q16. Airless spray, roller, or brush — which is best?
无气喷涂、滚涂还是刷涂？
+
Airless spray for large surfaces, fastest application, best film build. Roller for medium areas, textured finishes, repair work. Brush for small areas, edges, touch-ups. HONG NGUYEN products are optimized for airless spray (55:1 pump, .017–.023" tip).
